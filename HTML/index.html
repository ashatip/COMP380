Exponential Growth

Wearable Technology

According to Tractica, wearables are expected to become increasingly popular in the United States as we enter the next six years. The United States is on track to to have wearable device shipments of upwards of 174 million units by 2018.

As the market for fitness trackers is finally beginning to reach its peak market size, Tractica sees smartwatches now beginning to gain a significant platform in the wearable market space.

Tractica also notes that health-focused applications are now considered the next phase in the wearable market space moving forward
