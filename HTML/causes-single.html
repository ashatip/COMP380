Research

Smart wearable systems: Current status and future challenges, mentions how smart wearable systems can lower the global rising health care costs. The authors describe methods that can allow smart wearable systems to measure patients' health outside the hospital and then sends the doctor the data collected from the wearables. Furthermore, the article describes current and future obstacles that hinder smart wearable systems from integrating into the medical field.
